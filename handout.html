Getting Started With AI Workshop

Welcome to the UChicago Library’s first in our series of workshops on generative AI. The purpose of this handout is to give you helpful links you can consult after today if you want to continue exploring what we discuss here.

1. Contact Info

If you’d like to follow up about anything or have any questions, please feel free to get in touch with me at teichman@uchicago.edu.

2. Helpful Links

  • PhoenixAI: a version of ChatGPT that keeps your data private within UChicago campus (available at no charge to students, faculty, and staff): https://phoenixai.uchicago.edu/
  • Generative AI at UChicago https://genai.uchicago.edu/
  • UChicago Library LibGuide on AI: https://guides.lib.uchicago.edu/GenAI
  • Chatbot Arena (website for comparing different AI models): https://lmarena.ai/
  • Artificial Intelligence, an introductory overview by Melanie Mitchell: https://www.amazon.com/Artificial-Intelligence-audiobook/dp/B07YBHRX4B/
